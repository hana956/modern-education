Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access my child's test results?

You can access your child's test results by logging into our parent portal using the credentials provided during registration. If you haven't received login details, please contact our administrative office.

What are the school's admission requirements?

Admission requirements include a completed application form, previous academic records, a placement test, and an interview. Please visit our Admissions page for detailed information.

How can I schedule a meeting with a teacher?

Parent-teacher meetings can be scheduled through our online portal or by contacting the school office. We encourage parents to schedule meetings at least 48 hours in advance.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We follow all government health guidelines including regular sanitization, temperature checks, mask requirements, and social distancing in classrooms. Our full safety protocol is available on request.
